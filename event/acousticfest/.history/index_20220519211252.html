ACOUSTIC FEST 2022
ABOUT

Hetauda School of Management & Social Sciences is organizing its very first acoustic fest on March 26, 2022 at college premises, where students can perform (group/solo) using acoustic instruments.

The performer(s) can perform a maximum of 3 songs (max. 20 minutes), and repeated songs are restricted.

The program is set for March 26, 2022 at college premises, and will start from 5 PM.
